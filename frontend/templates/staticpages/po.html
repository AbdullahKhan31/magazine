The Provost

The functions of the Provost are to

  • promote the success of students at CIIT by providing enrichment programs, counseling, guidance, orientation, and support.
  • More specifically, he looks after the affairs of the student's hostels (male and female).
  • He also deals with students behavior issues and acts of indiscipline.
  • Promotes parent-teacher relationship, and supervises students activites.
